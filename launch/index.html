1 Executive Summary

The strategic case for launching Saint Lucia's first dedicated talent platform.

The Opportunity: Saint Lucia has no dedicated digital talent marketplace. Hiring relies on word-of-mouth, newspaper ads, and generic Caribbean job boards. With 90%+ of the population on Facebook and smartphone penetration growing rapidly, a mobile-first talent platform with video resumes enters a completely uncontested market.

What Is talent.stlucia?

A mobile-first Progressive Web App that lets Saint Lucian professionals and relocation talent create text + video resumes in under 2 minutes. Phase 1 collects talent profiles for free. Phase 2 monetizes through employer subscriptions ($99-$499/month) to search, filter, and contact candidates.

🎯

Value to Talent

"Get discovered by top employers. Submit your profile and video resume — we connect you with opportunities." Free forever for talent.

💼

Value to Employers

"Access Saint Lucia's largest pre-screened talent pool with video profiles. Hire faster, hire better." Subscription model starting at $99/mo.

⚡

Competitive Advantage

Only platform dedicated to SLU. Video resumes differentiate candidates. Mobile-first (where Saint Lucians are). Backed by stlucia.studio brand.

2 Product Overview

What the app does, how it's built, and what makes it special.

Core Features

  • 2-minute profile creation (name, skills, experience)
  • Video resume recording (90s, 5 guided prompts, front camera)
  • Email + Google OAuth authentication
  • Profile completeness scoring (0-100%)
  • PWA — installable on any phone
  • Referral system with unique codes
  • UTM + event tracking on all actions
  • PDF resume upload
  • Employer search & filter dashboard (Phase 2)

16 Risk Mitigation

Anticipated challenges and contingency plans.

HIGH RISK

Low Signup Rate

Risk: People visit but don't sign up. Target 20% conversion misses.

Mitigation: A/B test landing page copy. Reduce signup steps to just email + name. Add social proof ("X people joined today"). Offer incentive for first 100 signups.

HIGH RISK

Privacy/Trust Concerns

Risk: Users hesitant to record video or share personal data. Small island — fear of exposure.

Mitigation: Clear privacy policy (GDPR-level). Prominent data protection messaging. Video resume is optional. Users control visibility. Delete-anytime guarantee.

MEDIUM RISK

Video Recording Fails on Devices

Risk: MediaRecorder API doesn't work on older phones or Safari quirks.

Mitigation: Always offer upload alternative. Detect browser support before showing recorder. Test on 10+ device/browser combos. Graceful fallback to text-only profile.

MEDIUM RISK

No Employer Interest (Phase 2)

Risk: Employers don't see value in paying $99-499/mo. Prefer traditional recruitment.

Mitigation: Free trial (14 days). Start with just 1-2 anchor employers. Government partnership adds credibility. Prove ROI with placement data. Lower-cost entry tier if needed.

MEDIUM RISK

Ad Budget Exhaustion

Risk: $500 runs out before hitting 500 profiles target.

Mitigation: Prioritize organic channels (WhatsApp, TikTok, FB groups). Optimize CPC daily — pause underperforming ads. Lean into referral program. Partner with radio stations for free exposure.

LOW RISK

Technical Downtime

Risk: GitHub Pages or Supabase outage during launch.

Mitigation: PWA service worker caches core pages offline. Supabase has 99.9% SLA on Pro. UptimeRobot alerts. Cloudflare CDN provides edge caching layer. Static site = virtually no server errors.

Biggest Risk: Not launching fast enough. The longer the delay, the more a competitor can enter. The MVP philosophy is: launch with 80% product and iterate based on real user feedback. Speed over perfection.
