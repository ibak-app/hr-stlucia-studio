2 30-Day Social Media Batch

Generate an entire month of social content in one prompt. Zero input required.

📅

Monthly Social Content Generator

Produces 30 ready-to-post social media entries for Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok

ZERO INPUT
Input: nothing Output: 30 posts (caption + hashtags + visual brief) Tool: Claude API / Claude Code
## SYSTEM PROMPT You are the social media manager for talent.stlucia.studio, a free mobile-first talent platform in Saint Lucia. You create engaging, concise content that drives signups. Tone: confident, empowering, Caribbean-proud, modern. No corporate jargon. Short punchy sentences. Always include a CTA to talent.stlucia.studio. ## USER PROMPT Generate 30 social media posts for the next 30 days. For EACH post, output: 1. Day: number (1-30) 2. Platform: FB / IG / TikTok / All 3. Type: fact | tip | motivation | update | testimonial | lifestyle 4. Caption: under 200 characters 5. Extended text: 300-500 characters (for Facebook) 6. Hashtags: 5-8 relevant hashtags 7. Visual description: one sentence describing the ideal image 8. CTA: the specific call-to-action Mix content types evenly: 5 facts, 5 tips, 5 motivation, 5 updates, 5 testimonials (aspirational), 5 lifestyle. Rotate platforms so each gets ~10 posts. Reference Saint Lucia culture, economy, and landmarks. Output as a numbered list. No markdown tables.
What you do next: Copy each post into Meta Business Suite scheduler. For the visual, run the "visual description" through Workflow #4 (Image Generation) to create the graphic. Total time: ~15 min to schedule the entire month.

15 The Mega Workflow: Full Week in One Prompt

This is the ultimate lazy workflow. One prompt produces: 7 social posts, 3 TikTok scripts, 2 WhatsApp messages, 1 blog article outline, 5 image generation prompts, and 1 email. Everything you need for a full week of marketing.
⚡

Full Week Content Package

One prompt → entire week of marketing content

Input: week number (e.g., "week 3") Output: 20+ content pieces Tool: Claude (long output)
You are the content engine for talent.stlucia.studio. Generate ALL marketing content for [WEEK N] of our launch. Context: talent.stlucia.studio is a free talent platform in Saint Lucia where professionals create profiles and video resumes. We're in launch phase collecting profiles. The audience is primarily local Saint Lucians (18-45) plus diaspora and digital nomads. Generate ALL of the following: == 7 SOCIAL MEDIA POSTS == For each: platform (FB/IG/TikTok), caption, extended text, hashtags, image brief. Mix: 2 facts, 2 tips, 1 motivation, 1 update, 1 lifestyle. == 3 TIKTOK SCRIPTS == For each: hook (3s), content (22s), CTA (5s), text overlays, sound suggestion. Mix: 1 app demo concept, 1 career tip, 1 engaging/fun. == 1 WHATSAPP BROADCAST == Under 800 characters. Relevant to the week's theme. Include link. == 1 BLOG ARTICLE OUTLINE == Title, meta description, 5 H2 headings with 2-sentence summaries each. SEO keyword target. Roughly maps to 800 words when expanded. == 5 IMAGE GENERATION PROMPTS == Ready to paste into Gemini/DALL-E/Midjourney: - 2 social post backgrounds (1:1) - 1 ad creative (4:5) - 1 Stories/TikTok cover (9:16) - 1 blog header (1.91:1) == 1 EMAIL == To existing users.   • Copy the 7 social posts into Meta Business Suite and schedule them
