Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

talent.stlucia ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website talent.stlucia.studio (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile information: professional headline, skills, work experience, education, career summary, location, availability, desired roles
  • Video content: video resumes you record or upload through the Platform
  • Documents: PDF resumes and profile photos you upload
  • Communications: messages you send to us or through the Platform

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Platform:

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, actions taken
  • Device data: browser type, operating system, device type
  • Referral data: how you found the Platform (referral codes, UTM parameters)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your talent profile
  • Display your profile to potential employers (based on your settings)
  • Store and serve your video resumes and uploaded documents
  • Send you account-related communications (welcome emails, profile tips)
  • Improve the Platform and develop new features
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize the user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Public profile: Your profile information (excluding email and phone) is visible to employers and other users when your profile status is set to "active."
  • Employers: When employers access the platform (Phase 2), they may view your profile, skills, and video resume. Contact details are shared only with your consent or through our contact features.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for data storage and authentication, and may use additional services for analytics and email delivery. These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States (East region). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-level security on database tables
  • Secure authentication with hashed passwords
  • Access controls on file storage buckets

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent: You may stop using the Platform at any time and request account deletion

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@stlucia.studio.

6. Video Content

Video resumes you record or upload are stored in our secure storage system. Videos are associated with your profile and may be viewed by employers using the Platform. You may delete your video at any time from your profile settings. Deleted videos are permanently removed from our storage within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Authentication cookies: To keep you logged in to your account
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Plausible) that do not use cookies or track personal data
  • Referral tracking: We use URL parameters to track referral sources (stored in your browser's local storage)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

9. International Data

The Platform serves users in Saint Lucia and internationally. By using the Platform, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States where our infrastructure is hosted. We apply consistent data protection standards regardless of where data is processed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
